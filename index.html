👋 Welcome to Appcues!

Proudly empowering the product-led revolution—worldwide

Our story

Founded in 2013 by Jonathan Kim and Jackson Noel, Appcues set out to revolutionize the way software businesses onboard new users. We’ve since enabled thousands of businesses around the world to deliver beautiful and effective in-app experiences—without a developer. And our impact only continues to grow.

Today, Appcues is a complete solution for measuring and improving product adoption. Whether you’re a prospective customer, employee, investor, or friend (hi mom!), we look forward to sharing the rest of our journey with you.

The day-to-day

Appcues teammates hail from all over the map and enjoy many different interests, but we all share a love for blurple, our vibrant yet soothing brand color. We’re real big on customer happiness, and we celebrate our customers’ stunning flows every day in Slack—e.g. “this welcome modal is 🔥” or “❤️ this video in a slideout.”

Each morning, we sign into our respective tools, do our best work, and share puns and GIFs along the way. We take pride in the skills we bring to the table and strive to deliver excellent experiences for our customers, but we also don’t take ourselves too seriously.

Remote-first

The world changed in the spring of 2020, and so did we. Appcues is still relatively new to this #remote life, but we’re learning and growing every day and embracing the flexibility it offers. Love to code at 2am? Write your best emails with two animals on your lap? We’re here for you.

To keep us all connected, we’ve got Slack channels galore, questions of the day (Favorite recipe? Mullet or perm?), team lunches, holiday celebrations, quality swag (hello YETI cooler), two annual in-person retreats, and more.

We've got you covered

Rest easy and do your best work, knowing that we’ll take care of the important stuff… and provide some fun perks.

Well-being

You'll have a solid health, dental, and vision plan; access to 401k, and a generous family leave policy.

Home office and tech budget

We offer a $1,000 home office stipend, and an additional $500 annual budget for extra work-related technology.

Co-working space, on us

Home office not cutting it? We'll reimburse your monthly coworking fees.

Equity offerings

We want everyone invested in our success. Each employee is granted equity in the company.

Transparency and collaboration

We foster team alignment with meetings of all shapes and sizes—a monthly all-hands meeting called FirstThurs, ad hoc Lunch & Learns at team lunches, and more!

Flexible vacation

We believe time away to reflect and explore makes us all more productive, so we expect each employee to take a minimum of ten days of vacation per year.
